### KaTeX

Wrap a formula in `$` to display it as a block:

$`f{x} = \int_{-\infty}^\infty \hat f\xi\,e^{2 \pi i \xi x} \,d\xi`$

Multi-line block formulas are supported too:

```math
\begin{pmatrix}
   f(\alpha) & b        & c         \\
   e         & f(\beta) & g         \\
   i         & j        & f(\gamma)
\end{pmatrix}
```
